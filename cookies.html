Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 3/4 cup + 2 cups of brown sugar(I used brown sugar)
  • 1/2 cup or 112 grams of unsalted butter melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 whole egg and 1 egg yoke
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 7 ounces of dark chocolate(60-70% cacao) (could also use 7 ounces of chocolate chips)

Creating the dough

  1. Combine the required amount of sugar and brown sugar in stand mixer/bowl
  2. Turn stand mixer on and mix at low/medium speed until sugars are throughly combined
  3. Now grab your 1/2 cup of butter and melt it over low/medium heat on a pan
  4. Once fully melted and cooled pour your butter into your sugar mixture
  5. Mix the butter into the sugar on low until it looks like this

  6. Now add your 1 whole egg and egg yolk into your mixture
  7. Once fully mixed now add in your vanilla extract and mix until fully combined
  8. Now add your flour, baking soda, and salt to the mixture
  9. Mix for an additional 5 minutes on medium. It should take on a dough form
  10. Now mix your chocolate chips into your dough with a rubber spatula or wooden spatula

  11. Once fully combine store in the fridge for 30-45 minutes

Folding The Dough And Baking The Cookies:

  1. Once the dough is chilled grab a metal spoon and scoop the dough out like so

  2. Grab the dough from the spoon and using a rolling motion roll the dough into a ball. While doing this preheat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Line a baking tray and repeat steps 1-2 in this section until you have around 6 evenly sized balls. Then space them out around 1.5-2 inches away from each other on your lined baking tray.
  4. Bake for around 8-10 minutes at 350 degrees Fahrenheit. (Warning: Cook time can vary from cookie size).
  5. (Optional step) Every 4-6 minutes with oven mittens grab the tray and drop the tray back down. (This will flatten the cookie's air bubble that is formed from baking). This will provide a more flat cookie. Additional instructions can be accessed in this Article:The Ideal Chocolate Chip Cookie + A Cookie Hack By Joshua Weissman at around 4:00 into the video.
  6. After cooling for 5 minutes you can now enjoy your cookies!
